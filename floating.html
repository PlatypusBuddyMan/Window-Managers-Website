Floating/Stacking Window Managers

Floating window managers a.k.a. stacking window managers are the most commonly used window managers. They are used in Microsoft Windows, MacOS, and most major Linux distributions. Floating/Stacking window managers allow windows to overlap each other. Windows in floating/stacking window managers float in their own spot on screen. Typically designed to be used with both mouse & keyboard.
